Careers

If you are a CS student or you are studying on your own you need to develop your skills and gain first commercial experience in order to become a professional. We require tech skills which are necessary however your level of independence is the most important for us. We are ready to help you but your will to learn is a priority.

We are currently looking for candidates for the position of:

Frontend Developer

Requirements:

  1. HTML, CSS
  2. JavaScript (ES6)
  3. At least one framework JS, (we prefer React)
  4. Git

We offer:

  1. Soon (3 - 4 months) we are going to transform our team into a company and you will work with us formally.
  2. You will be able to gain credits which are essential for your CV.
  3. Your programming skills will improve due to wide range of our jobs

Backend Developer

Requirements:

  1. Git
  2. Any programming language with responding backend framework.
  3. relational databases, SQL.

We offer:

  1. Soon (3 - 4 months) we are going to transform our team into a company and you will work with us formally.
  2. Your programming skills will improve due to wide range of our jobs

Web Designer

We offer:

  1. Soon (3 - 4 months) we are going to transform our team into a company and you will work with us formally.

DevOps
